## Threat Actor Types (Beginner-Friendly)

* **Script Kiddies:** Use simple tools and scripts, often experimenting or causing minor disruptions
* **Cybercriminals:** Focused on financial gain, such as stealing data or credentials
* **Insider Threats:** Employees or users misusing legitimate access
* **Hacktivists:** Attackers motivated by social or political goals

> *Note: This section is for awareness only; no offensive actions are performed.*
